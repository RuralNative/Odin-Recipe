Adobong Manok - Pinoy Version

Chicken adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines that is often considered as the country's national dish. It is a savory, tangy, and slightly sweet stew made with chicken that has been marinated in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and other spices.

Chicken adobo is typically served over steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions or chopped cilantro. It can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish, and is often accompanied by other Filipino favorites like lumpia (spring rolls) or pancit (noodles).

Overall, chicken adobo is a beloved dish in the Philippines and is loved for its simple yet flavorful taste, making it a popular choice for casual meals and special occasions alike.
